Lateral length (ft), scraped and calculated from "scout cards" of wells,
accessed from the ECMC's website.

Interval Top: xxxx ft.
Interval Bottom: xxxx ft.
Measured TD: xxxx ft. (total depth)

Lateral length is [interval bottom] - [interval top], if both are available.
Measured TD is used in place of interval bottom where the bottom is not
specified in the scout card.
